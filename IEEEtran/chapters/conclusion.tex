\section{Conclusion}

This paper investigated the theoretical and technical requirements for a biofeedback system designed for stress reduction and consciousness regulation.Based on the research, the CHROM algorithm proved to be the most suitable solution for heart rate detection. It is efficient to implement, requires minimal hardware resources, and provides robust results even when dealing with motion and lighting artifacts. The transformation of the data to sound is most effective when the principles of ecological perception are taken into account. An organic soundscape is necessary to ensure an intuitive and pleasant user experience. In summary, while this work is based on a theoretical analysis of existing methods, it provides a clear roadmap for future implementations of camera-based closed-loop systems.
