\section{Conclusion}

This paper investigated the theoretical and technical requirements for a biofeedback system designed for stress reduction and consciousness regulation.Based on the research, the CHROM algorithm proved to be the most suitable solution for heart rate detection. It is efficient to implement, requires minimal hardware resources, and provides robust results even when dealing with motion and lighting artifacts. The transformation of the data to sound is most effective when the principles of ecological perception are taken into account.
In addition, it is necessary that the music percipitates a pleasant user experience. This work only researched the theoretical framework for such an implementation but comes to the conclusion that such a system should be realistically feasible.
